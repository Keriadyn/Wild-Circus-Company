Anna Derevko

Born in Russia, Anna was known thanks to the trapeze in big swinging. She's a model for the new generations. We had been able to applaud her in 2014.
Her sequences of movements on the trapeze amazed us. The quality of its work moreover cost her a silver medal from the festival of the Circus of Tomorrow.
Since then, Anna pursued her artistic evolution with one copy versatility. She managed to combine two disciplines: the trapeze and the antipodisme.

The three Nukimura

Kira, Asran and Flay Nukimura intervene twice in the show.

- The unstable board
The material(equipment) consists of a cylindrical roller and a board. The preservation of the balance requires legs movements moderate and brief.
With their make-up and their suits, the Nukimura transports us in Federico Fellini's universe. Their technique is irreproachable. They realize even a column in three on roller. Not amazing that they obtained the bronze medal to the festival of Monte Carlo in 2013.

- The Carried Pole
The carrier keeps his pole in vertical position. The support of the device can be made on the shoulder, the forehead, the single arm or the support of belt.
The equipment has to answer two requirements: the lightness and the solidity.
Nukimura was a part of some troops Japanese little known before joining Wild Circus.
